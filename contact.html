Quick Answers

Common Questions

How long does an edit take? +
Most projects are delivered within 48–72 hours. Longer videos (30+ min) may take 4–5 business days. Rush delivery is available on request.
What files do I need to send? +
Raw footage, any assets (music, logos, references), and a brief. I'll guide you through anything else we need.
How many revisions do I get? +
Unlimited revisions within scope until you're satisfied. I don't deliver until the edit is right.
What software do you use? +
Primarily Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and DaVinci Resolve. Also fluent in CapCut for short-form content.
Do you handle music licensing? +
Yes — I source from royalty-free libraries (Epidemic Sound, Artlist) and can advise on licensing for your platform and use case.
